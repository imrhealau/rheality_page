    Verdict & provenance

    Demo note. This dashboard is Rheality's monitoring front-end. The displacement series shown is reconstructed from the published post-event analysis of Dam I (Grebby et al. 2021, Communications Earth & Environment), which found sub-36 mm/yr face creep accelerating anomalously about 40 days before the 25 January 2019 collapse. In production the same panels read a displacement table generated by our own InSAR pipeline from free Sentinel-1 radar. It is shown here to demonstrate the product and the alerting logic, not as an original processing result.
