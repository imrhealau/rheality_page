Our mission · Hong Kong × Space

The revenue side of the space industry in Asia.

Most of the world has no space programme — and the capability they need is being built on both sides of the Pacific. Our mission: open space to the nations still outside it. Rheality gets space-data companies their first APAC revenue, and helps emerging space nations tap the best of both worlds — from Hong Kong, the harbour built to face East and West at once.

What we do

🚀

Market entry

Your first signed deal in Taiwan, the Philippines, Indonesia, Hong Kong, or the wider region — buyer identification, first meetings, negotiation, and close. Retainer plus success fee: we only win if you do.

🧭

Procurement & licensing navigation

APAC buyers want the data but can't procure from Western vendors without a local counterpart — and vendors can't navigate the rules back. We come from inside this world — Hong Kong engineering and a €2B geo-data firm — and we run the maze for you.

🛰️

Distribution that compounds

One market becomes a playbook; the playbook becomes a channel. We're building the distribution and licensing layer between space-data suppliers and Asia-Pacific buyers — so selling into the region stops being a bespoke project.

Why now

The buyer side of Asia is opening — fast.

Hong Kong has mandated its sovereign investment corporation to invest in the commercial space economy and is reforming LEO satellite licensing. Regional infrastructure, resources, and insurance buyers already spend on geospatial data — they just can't reach Western suppliers. The supply side is commoditizing exactly as the demand side switches on.

The companies that win Asia-Pacific won't be the ones with the best satellite. They'll be the ones in the room. And the nations building space capability from zero shouldn't have to pick a side to get started — that's the gap Hong Kong was made to fill.
