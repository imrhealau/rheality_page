Why now

The Buyer Side of Asia Is Opening — Fast.

Hong Kong has mandated its sovereign investment corporation to invest in the commercial space economy and is reforming LEO satellite licensing. Regional infrastructure, resources, and insurance buyers already spend on geospatial data — they just can't reach Western suppliers. The supply side is commoditizing exactly as the demand side switches on.

The companies that win Asia-Pacific won't be the ones with the best satellite. They'll be the ones in the room. And the nations building space capability from zero shouldn't have to pick a side to get started — that's the gap Hong Kong was made to fill.
