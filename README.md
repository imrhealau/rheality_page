# Rheality

Independent satellite verification for physical-asset risk in Asia. We monitor ground and structural movement from orbit (tailings dams, slopes, reclamation, data centres, LNG and rail) and deliver it as an auditable monitoring subscription with alerts and compliance-ready reports. We buy the radar; the product is the analytics and the independent verification on top of it.
